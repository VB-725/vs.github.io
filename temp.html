About Shri Umashiva Kshethra

Shri Umashiva Kshethra, nestled in the serene town of Kalladka, is a divine temple dedicated to Lord Shiva and Goddess Uma. The temple has a rich history of devotion and cultural heritage, attracting thousands of pilgrims every year. With its peaceful atmosphere and beautiful surroundings, the temple offers an ideal place for prayer, meditation, and spiritual rejuvenation.
Kalladka is a small developing town near the Mangalore to Bangalore National Highway 75. The Temple is located about 200 hundred meters away from this highway, surrounded by humongous trees and a peaceful atmosphere. About 150 years ago the Kuplucharu Family built a dormitary for tourists and students. 100 years ago, Mari Bhat established the UmaMaheshwara Linga at this place brought from Kashi city. The temple management was handed over to the Ramachandrapura Mutt 50 years ago. In 2006, the temple was renovated. The temple is surrounded by forest in about 3 acres of land.The chirping of birds and the shadows from the huge trees, provide a peaceful and calm environment in the area.
The specility of this temple is that during Full Moon to New moon, Sri UmaMaheshwara Linga turns black and again turns white during Full Moon.
